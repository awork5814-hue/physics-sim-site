Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Physics Experiments Lab ("we", "our", or "us") collects information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and payment information when you subscribe
  • Quiz Responses: Your answers to physics questions and quiz performance data
  • Simulation Usage: Which simulations you use and how you interact with them
  • Communication Data: Messages you send us through contact forms

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent
  • Achievement Data: Progress on learning achievements and badges
  • Preferences: Theme settings, display preferences stored locally

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide subscription services and process payments through Paymob (EGP) and PayTabs (USD)
  • Track your learning progress and award achievements
  • Personalize your experience with simulations and questions
  • Send important updates about your subscription
  • Improve our services based on usage patterns
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

3. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We share your data with:

3.1 Payment Processing (Paymob, PayTabs)

Your payment information is processed by Paymob (EGP) and PayTabs (USD), our payment service providers. We do not store your complete credit card details on our servers. These providers may process your data according to their privacy policies.

3.2 Data Processing

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (to understand how our site is used)
  • Hosting and infrastructure
  • Customer support

These services have access to your data only as needed to perform their functions and are obligated to protect it.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time. We may retain certain information for legal compliance purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email provided on our contact page.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Store your learning progress and achievements
  • Keep you logged into your account

These are essential for the functionality of our service and cannot be disabled without affecting your experience.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure payment processing through Paymob and PayTabs
  • Access controls on our servers
  • Regular security assessments

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Egypt. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
