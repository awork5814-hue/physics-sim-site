F = -kx
📚 Theory

Hooke's Law states that the force in an ideal spring is proportional to displacement: F = -kx. The negative sign means the force is restoring.

Elastic potential energy: PE = 1/2 kx²

🔬 Investigation Questions
  1. Keep mass constant. Double k. How does force change for x = 0.3 m?
  2. Set damping = 0. Start oscillation. Does total energy stay constant?
  3. Keep k constant. Double mass. How does period change?
  4. Keep mass constant. Double k. How does period change?
💡 Key Takeaways
  • Linear relationship: F ∝ x for ideal springs.
  • Restoring force always opposes displacement.
  • Energy stored in the spring: PE = 1/2 kx².
  • Period: T = 2π√(m/k).
