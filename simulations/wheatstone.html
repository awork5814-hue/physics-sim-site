📚 Experiment Explanation

A Wheatstone bridge compares two voltage dividers. When the ratios match, the midpoint potentials are equal, the galvanometer reads zero, and the bridge is balanced.

Balance condition: R₁/R₂ = R₃/Rₓ. Use the sliders to change the resistors and watch the galvanometer needle. Press Balance to auto‑adjust R₃ until the needle centers.

This method is used to measure an unknown resistance Rₓ with high precision by balancing the bridge.
