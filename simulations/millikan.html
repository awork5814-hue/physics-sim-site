Millikan Oil Drop Experiment

Robert Millikan measured the charge of single electrons using suspended oil droplets.

How it works:

• Oil droplets are sprayed between parallel plates

• An electric field opposes gravity

• When balanced, qE = mg

• Measure charge by timing droplet motion

Goal: Find the greatest common divisor of measured charges to determine e
