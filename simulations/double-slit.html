Interference pattern
📚 Theory

Double slit interference creates a pattern of bright and dark fringes due to constructive and destructive interference.

🔬 Investigation Questions
  1. What happens to fringe spacing if you increase slit separation?
  2. How does wavelength affect fringe spacing?
  3. Why do fringes fade away from the center?
💡 Key Takeaways
  • Fringe spacing ∝ λ and inversely ∝ d.
  • Central maximum is brightest.
  • Single-slit envelope dims outer fringes.
