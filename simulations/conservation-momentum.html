Conservation of Momentum

Total momentum before = total momentum after

📚 Theory

Momentum p = mv is conserved in isolated systems. Kinetic energy is conserved only in elastic collisions.

🔬 Investigation Questions
  1. Calculate total momentum before and after. Is it conserved?
  2. How does energy change in inelastic collisions?
  3. What happens if one mass is much larger?
💡 Key Takeaways
  • Momentum always conserved (no external force).
  • Elastic collisions conserve KE.
  • Inelastic collisions lose KE.
