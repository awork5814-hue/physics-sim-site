Lorentzian (Cauchy) Distribution

The Lorentzian (or Cauchy) distribution is a symmetric probability distribution with heavy tails.

Key difference from Gaussian:

• Mean and variance are undefined!
• Tails decay as 1/x² (much slower than Gaussian)
• Used in physics for spectral lines and resonance

Formula: f(x) = (1/πγ) × γ²/(γ² + (x-x₀)²)

Adjust x₀ to move the peak, γ to change the width (FWHM = 2γ)
