Uniform Circular Motion

Constant speed • Centripetal acceleration

📚 Theory

Uniform circular motion has constant speed but continuously changing velocity direction. The acceleration always points toward the center.

🔬 Investigation Questions
  1. Is speed constant? Is velocity constant?
  2. What happens to a when radius doubles?
  3. What happens to a when speed doubles?
💡 Key Takeaways
  • v = 2πr / T.
  • a = v² / r = ω²r.
  • Force is always toward the center.
