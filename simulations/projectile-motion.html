Projectile Motion

Parabolic trajectory under gravity

Motion Graphs

x(t), y(t), vx(t), vy(t)
📚 Theory

Projectile motion combines constant horizontal velocity with constant downward acceleration. The path is a parabola.

🔬 Investigation Questions
  1. Try 30° and 60°. Which gives the same range?
  2. What happens to vx at the top of the trajectory?
  3. Is 45° always optimal for range?
💡 Key Takeaways
  • Horizontal and vertical motion are independent.
  • vx is constant, vy changes linearly.
  • Range depends on sin(2θ) for level ground.
