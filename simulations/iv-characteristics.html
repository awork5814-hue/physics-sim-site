I-V Characteristics

Current–Voltage relationships for circuit components

📚 Theory

I–V characteristics show how current responds to applied voltage. Ohmic conductors are linear, non-ohmic components are curved.

🔬 Investigation Questions
  1. Is the resistor graph a straight line?
  2. At V = 0.5 V, does a diode conduct?
  3. Why does a filament lamp curve upward?
💡 Key Takeaways
  • Resistor: linear I-V.
  • Diode: exponential, one-way.
  • LED: diode with color-dependent forward voltage.
  • Slope gives conductance.
