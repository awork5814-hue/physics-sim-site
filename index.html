About This Lab

Physics Experiments Lab is an interactive platform featuring 156 simulations across 20 physics domains—from classical mechanics and fluid dynamics to quantum physics and astrophysics. Each experiment includes adjustable parameters, real-time visualization, guided questions, and theory explanations. The goal is to make fundamental physics concepts tangible through hands-on exploration rather than static diagrams.
