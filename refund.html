Refund Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Overview

We want you to be satisfied with your subscription. This policy explains how refunds work with Physics Experiments Lab.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

2.1 Time Limit

Refund requests must be submitted within 90 days from the date of purchase. After 90 days, refunds cannot be processed except in cases recognized by international payment networks (Visa, MasterCard).

2.2 Refund Conditions

Refunds may be requested for:

  • Technical Issues: If our service is not accessible due to technical problems on our end
  • Billing Errors: If you were charged incorrectly or duplicate charges occurred
  • Service Not as Described: If the service differs significantly from what was advertised
  • Unauthorized Charges: If charges appear that you did not authorize

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us through our contact form or email
  2. Provide your account email and order reference number
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Allow 3-5 business days for us to review your request

Refund requests can be submitted to: awork5814@gmail.com

4. Refund Processing

4.1 Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days. The refund will be credited to the original payment method used for the purchase.

4.2 Payment Method

Refunds will be processed to:

  • Card Payments: Refunded to the card used for the original transaction
  • Mobile Wallets: Refunded to the wallet used for payment
  • Kiosk Payments: Refunded to the original payment method

Note: The payment method must still be active and accessible for the refund to be processed.

5. Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, partial refunds may be available:

  • Annual Subscriptions: If you request a refund mid-subscription, you may receive a refund for unused months
  • Multiple Charges: If duplicate charges occurred, excess charges will be refunded

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Refunds requested after 90 days from purchase
  • Subscriptions that have been fully utilized
  • Refunds to payment methods that are no longer active
  • Charges for services already rendered

7. Automatic Refunds

In some cases, automatic refunds may occur:

  • Duplicate Transactions: If we detect duplicate charges, the excess will be automatically refunded
  • Payment Disputes: If a dispute is resolved in your favor through Paymob, PayTabs, or your bank

8. Subscription Cancellation

To cancel your subscription and avoid future charges:

  1. Log into your account settings
  2. Navigate to subscription management
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Confirm the cancellation

Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

9. Payment Provider Policies

Payments are processed through Paymob (EGP) and PayTabs (USD). By using our service, you agree to their terms and acknowledge that:

  • Providers may have separate refund processing timelines
  • Some refunds may be subject to provider policies
  • Provider processing fees are generally non-refundable

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request:

Email: awork5814@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 48 hours.
